# Model selection
This section is to decide what will be the brain for agents.
thee system could be single or multi agent. selecting the LLM based on business use case is very critical.
this needs understanding of the Goals , role and skills of each agent. depending upon the pattern , context window , memory we need to decide what is the right model.
there are enterprise models form openAI , Anthropic , google , deepseek , groq ,etc. and there are open source models from hugging face , Nvidia NIM ,etc.
its important to consider all kind of models and their benchmark and map those to business cases.
every agent may ahve its own LLM, it could even have more than one , which can be routed based on runtime.
while selecting the model , its benchmark like MMLU (knowledge classification) , DocVQA , GPQA (graduate reasoning) , MT-Bench (writing quality) , Berkeley Function Calling Leaderboard (BFCL) , SWE-bench Verified , HotpotQA (multi-hop) , MMMU (multimodal understanding) , MT-Bench (multi-turn)  ,etc must be considered
Another important aspect of the models is what will be cost of hosting open source LLMs or using enterprise llm with token usage.
hosting a open source model incur Hardware cost.
there may be an agent which does very simple and not so critical task , such agents can use cheapest or even distilled small models. but that must come with right considerations.
Businesds KPIs , compliance and regulations , sunny and rainy day scenarios goals and objectives are very important consideration while selecting the model.
